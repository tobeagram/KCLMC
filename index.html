If you have any questions or queries feel free to send us an email.

London University Bouldering Event

LUBE is a student run, not for profit, national bouldering competition. It is open to any university teams wishing to compete. It was started in 2010 in response to the lack of affordable bouldering competitions aimed at student teams. LUBE has come a long way since the initial single round competition to a 4 round annual competition across the Autumn and Winter months.

LUBE takes place over 4 rounds at prominent venues across London. Each university can bring up to 8 climbers (2x teams of three members (mixed) and 2 individuals). 25 routes are set out and climbers are given three hours to complete as many routes as they can. Prizes are awarded to the top three men and women at each round and the overall top three men, women and university teams at the final round.

Climb with Us

Not sure where to go climbing? If you are part of KCL you are welcome to join us for a free taster session. Keep your ears to the ground, we run them in January and September of every year. Want to climb now? Send us a message on our facebook page! See below for the climbing centres we currently go to:


Wednesday - Westway

The Westway Sports Centre is nestled in-between a knot of motorways in North Kensington and is home to some of the best lead climbing in Central London.

There is a hardcore bouldering area known as The Fridge (V2 and above) for the more avid climber. You won't see any jugs in there, at least any climbing ones.

On Wednesday we all travel there together from Macadam, arriving from 2pm onwards. With sports night later on, it is the most thought about day of the week!


Friday - Building One

One of the three climbing walls around London by the Arch Climbing. It is undoubtably one of the best bordering walls in London.

Building One is the home to our bouldering team who will be there most days, sweating out all of the stress from uni. If you are a member of KCLMC, entry is only ţ with discounts on seasonal passes.

On Fridays we all climb together from 5pm, often with a social to the local pub afterwards. Its a great way to have a relaxed Friday evening.
